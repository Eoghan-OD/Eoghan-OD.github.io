What Encryptix Provides for You

Discover the practical services and protections Encryptix brings to veterinary clinics and animal shelters. Click to reveal how we support your security needs.

Staff Training & Awareness

We teach your team how encryption works, how to detect digital threats, and how to maintain safe data handling practices in a busy clinical environment.

Data Encryption Setup

We configure strong encryption for client records, payment information, medical histories, and internal communications to keep your data protected at all times.

Secure Communications

Encryptix helps implement encrypted channels for email, referrals, and inter-practice communication so sensitive information cannot be intercepted.

Compliance Assistance

We guide your clinic through GDPR and Irish Data Protection requirements, ensuring your encryption practices meet legal and ethical standards.
